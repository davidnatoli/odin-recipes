Black Bean Quinoa Tacos

  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup dry qunioa

  • Corn tortillas
  • salsa of choice
  • sliced avocado

  2. Toast the aromatics

  5. Assemble & serve
