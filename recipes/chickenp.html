Chicken Parmesean

Description:

"My version of chicken Parmesan is a little different than what they do in the restaurants, with less sauce and a crispier crust." - Chef John

Ingredients:
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzerella
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Season Chicken with salt and pepper
  3. beat eggs in shallow bowl
  4. Mix bread crumbs and parmesean cheese
  5. Flour chicken breasts
  6. Dip chicken breasts in eggs and press both sides in crumb mixture
  7. Heat 1/2 inch olive oil in skillet and sear chicken on both sides
  8. Transfer chicken to baking dish and top with tomato sauce and mozzerella
  9. Bake until 165 degrees
